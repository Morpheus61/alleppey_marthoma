# How to Use — St. George Marthoma Church App
### A Plain-Language Guide for Members, Group Leaders & Admins

*Last updated: 2026-07-23*

---

## SECTION 1 — For Every Parish Member

### How to Sign In

**First time using the app:**
1. Enter your mobile number and tap **Continue**
2. A 6-digit code is sent to your phone by SMS — enter that code
3. Choose a **password** (at least 8 characters — letters and numbers are fine)
4. From now on, you only need your mobile number and password. No more SMS codes.

**If you have signed in before:**
1. Enter your mobile number and tap **Continue**
2. Enter your password and tap **Sign In**

**If you got a new phone:** Just enter your number and password as usual. No code is needed.

**Forgot your password?**
1. Enter your mobile number and tap **Continue**
2. Tap **Forgot password?** (shown below the password box)
3. A 6-digit code is sent to your phone — enter it
4. Choose a new password (at least 8 characters)

---

### First Time? Please Fill In Your Details

After you first sign in, your account will say **"Waiting for Approval"**. The church office needs to approve you before you can use the full app.

**While you wait, please fill in your details — this helps the office approve you faster:**

1. Tap **Profile** (at the bottom of the screen on a phone, or on the left side on a computer)
2. At the top you will see two photo buttons:
   - **Profile Picture** (round) — tap to add your own photo
   - **Family Photo** (wide rectangle) — tap to add a family group photo
   - *(Photos save on their own — you do not need to press Save for photos)*
3. Fill in your details:
   - Your full name in English
   - Your full name in Malayalam *(tap the "Type → മലയാളം" button — the app will try to spell it out; please check and correct the Malayalam yourself)*
   - Date of Birth
   - House Name / Family Name
   - Your address
   - Landline number (if you have one)
   - WhatsApp number (or tick "My mobile is my WhatsApp number")
   - Email address
4. Add your **Family Members** — tap "Add Family Member" for each person in your home:
   - Their name (English and Malayalam)
   - Their relation to you (Spouse, Son, Daughter, etc.)
   - Their date of birth
5. Tap **"Save Details"** — a green tick ✓ will appear to confirm it has saved

Once the Admin approves your account, you will have full access to the app.

---

### Finding Your Way Around

**On a phone:** Look at the **bottom of the screen** — you will see a row of icons.
**On a computer or tablet:** Look at the **left side of the screen** — the menu is there.

| Label | What It Opens |
|---|---|
| 🏠 **Home** | Welcome page with announcements and upcoming events |
| 👥 **Groups** | All parish groups (prayer groups, committees, fellowships) |
| 📖 **Directory** | The parish member list with photos |
| 📅 **Calendar** | Upcoming church events and services |
| 👛 **Finance** | Your dues, payment history, and pay options |
| 👤 **Profile** | Your own details, photos, and family members |
| 🛡 **Admin** | Church office controls *(only visible to office staff)* |

---

### Viewing Your Groups

1. Tap **Groups**
2. You will see a list of all active parish groups
3. Tap any group to see more details and who leads it
4. If you are a member of that group, tap **"View Feed"** to read messages from the leader

---

### Reading Group Messages (Feed)

- You can only read a group's messages if you are **an approved member** of that group
- Messages show **Malayalam first** (if the leader wrote it), then English below
- 📌 Pinned messages always appear at the very top
- If it says "Join this group to see its feed", ask your Group Leader or the church office to add you

---

### The Parish Directory

- Tap **Directory** to see all active parish members
- Each person's card shows their **family photo** and their **profile picture**
- Tap the green WhatsApp button to start a chat with them
- Use the search box to find someone by name, house name, or address

---

### Notifications (Event Reminders)

- The app can remind you about upcoming events
- When the app asks permission for notifications, tap **"Allow"**
- You will get a reminder message before events that involve you

---

### How to Sign Out

- **On a phone:** Tap **Profile** → tap the sign-out arrow (→|) in the top-right corner
- **On a computer:** The sign-out button is in the top-right corner of the screen at all times

## SECTION 2 — For Group Leaders (Convenors)

*As a Group Leader you can do everything a regular member can, plus you can manage your group.*

### Opening Your Group's Management Page

1. Tap **Groups**
2. Find your group and tap it
3. You will see a **"Manage Group"** button at the top (only you can see this button)
4. Tap it to open the management area

---

### Posting a Message to Your Group

1. Open your Group Management page
2. In the **"Post Message"** box:
   - Type your message in **English** on the left side, OR in **Malayalam** on the right side, OR in **both**
   - At least one language must have something written — a Malayalam-only message is perfectly fine
3. If you want help writing the Malayalam, type the English first and click **"Draft →"**
   - ⚠️ The app converts the letters sound by sound — this is NOT a translation. You must read the result carefully and rewrite it in proper Malayalam
   - Tick the box **"I have read the Malayalam"** before moving on
4. Click **"Preview & Post →"**
5. You will see exactly what your group members will see — if it looks right, click **"Send"**

---

### Approving Someone Who Wants to Join Your Group

When a member asks to join, a **"Join Requests"** section will appear on your Manage page:

- Tap **"Approve"** to add them to the group
- Tap **"Decline"** to say no

---

### Seeing and Removing Group Members

On the Manage page, scroll down to **"Active Members"** to see everyone in your group.

- Tap **"Remove"** to remove someone from the group (they can ask to rejoin later)

> **Please note:** Only the church office (Admin) can appoint or remove Group Leaders. You cannot change another member's leader status.

---

### What Group Leaders Cannot Do

- Create or delete groups — only the church office can do this
- Appoint or remove other Group Leaders — only the church office can do this
- Change a member's name, phone number, or account status — only the church office can do this
- Approve new parish members — only the church office can do this
- See another group's management page

## SECTION 3 — For the Church Office (Admins)

*The church office has full access and control over the entire app.*

### The Admin Dashboard

1. Sign in with your office account
2. Tap **Admin** in the top bar — this link only appears for office staff
3. The dashboard shows you:
   - How many active members there are
   - Who is waiting for approval
   - How many groups are active
   - Events happening this week

---

### Approving a New Member

When someone registers, they appear under **"Pending Approvals"** on the Admin Dashboard:

1. Check their name and phone number
2. Tap **"Approve"** — they immediately get full access
3. Tap **"Decline"** — their account is turned off

> New members **cannot use the app at all** until someone from the office approves them.

---

### Adding a Member Yourself (Without Them Registering)

1. Go to the **Directory** page → find the **"Add Single Member"** section
2. Fill in: Full Name, Mobile Number (10 digits, required), House Name, and Status
3. Tap **"Add Member"**

The member's account will be ready when they first sign in with that mobile number.

---

### Turning Off a Member's Account

From the **Directory**:
1. Find the member in the active list
2. Tap the **red 🚧 icon** next to their name
3. Confirm when asked

To **turn it back on**: scroll to the bottom of the Directory → tap the **green ✓ icon** next to their name.

---

### Editing a Member's Details

1. **Directory** → find the member → tap the **✏️ pencil icon**
2. Change whatever is needed: name, address, family members, status
3. Status choices:
   - **Active** — normal access
   - **Pending** — puts them back to "waiting for approval"
   - **Disabled** — blocks their access completely
4. Tap **"Save Details"**

---

### Creating a New Group

1. On the Admin Dashboard, find the **"Groups"** section
2. Fill in:
   - **Group Name** in English
   - **Group Name** in Malayalam — optional
   - **Short web name** — a simple version of the name for the web address, e.g. `prayer-group` or `youth-fellowship` *(no spaces — use hyphens instead)*
   - **Type**: Functional / Prayer / Youth
3. Tap **"Create Group"**
4. The group appears in the list straight away

---

### Closing a Group That Is No Longer Active

1. Admin Dashboard → Groups
2. Find the group and tap **"Archive"**
3. The group is hidden from members but all its records are kept safely

To bring a group back, tap **"Unarchive"**.

---

### Posting a Parish-Wide Announcement

1. Admin Dashboard → **"Post Announcement"**
2. Type in **English** and/or **Malayalam** — at least one is required
3. Click **"Preview & Post →"** — check how it will look to members
4. Click **"Send"** to post it

---

### Appointing a Group Leader (Convenor)

1. Admin Dashboard → Groups → tap **Manage** for the group
2. Scroll to **"Members"**
3. Find the person → tap **"Make Leader"**
4. To remove their leader role: tap **"Revoke Leader"**

> Only the church office can appoint or remove Group Leaders. A Group Leader cannot give leader access to another member.

---

### Setting Up the Vicar's Account

The Vicar signs in the same way as any other member — with their mobile number and password. The app gives them full Super Admin access based on the roles list.

To set up the Vicar's access after they first register:
**Admin → Roles → Select the Vicar's name → Vicar (Super Admin) → Grant Role**

---

### Giving Roles to Staff (Secretary, Treasurer, Deacon)

1. Go to **Admin → Roles** — only the Vicar can do this
2. Choose the staff member from the list
3. Choose their role: Deacon / Treasurer / Secretary (Admin) / Vicar (Super Admin)
4. Tap **"Grant Role"**

> Every role change is permanently recorded. Roles can be revoked but the record stays.

---

### Importing Many Members at Once

1. Go to **Directory → Import Members**
2. Prepare a spreadsheet with columns: `full_name`, `phone`, `house_name`
3. Upload the file
4. All imported members start as **pending** — their accounts become active when they first sign in

---

### Important Reminders for Office Staff

- **Approve new members quickly** — they cannot use the app at all while waiting
- **Keep the Groups list tidy** — archive groups that are no longer meeting
- **Do not disable an account without speaking to the member first**
- **The directory is only visible to approved active members**

## SECTION 4 — For the Vicar (Super Admin)

### The Approvals Queue

When the Secretary, Treasurer, or Deacon makes certain changes, those changes need the Vicar's approval before they take effect. They all appear here.

1. Go to **Admin → Approvals**
2. Each item shows who made the request and exactly what they want to change (before and after)
3. Tap **"✓ Approve"** to apply the change immediately, or write a reason and tap **"✕ Reject"**

> Changes the Vicar makes directly are applied immediately — no approval needed.

---

## SECTION 5 — Parish Registry (Church Office / Vicar)

### Adding a Household

Go to **Admin → Registry → + Add Household** → fill in the house name (English and Malayalam), address, and Bhagam → tap Save.

All family members linked to this household will automatically be added to that Bhagam's prayer group.

### Adding Family Members to a Household

Open the household card → tap **+ Add Member** → fill in name, relation, date of birth, and gender.

### Recording a Life Event (Baptism, Confirmation, Marriage, Death)

Open the household → find the person → tap **Record Event** → choose the event type → fill in the date, place, officiant's name, and any register or certificate numbers → tap Save.

> Life events cannot be edited after saving. If a correction is needed, record a new corrected entry — both records are kept together.

---
---

## SECTION 6 — Finance (Deacon, Treasurer, Church Office)

### Recording a Cash Payment (Deacon)

Go to **Admin → Finance → Record Cash** → search for the family → choose the collection type → enter the amount → tap Record.
The receipt number appears straight away and the family is notified.

### Checking and Confirming a Bank/UPI Payment (Treasurer)

Go to **Admin → Finance → Verify Payments** → compare the payment reference and any screenshot against your bank statement → tap **Verify** (to issue a receipt) or **Reject** (with a reason).

### Creating a New Collection (Vicar)

Go to **Admin → Finance → Collections → + New Collection** → fill in the fund, name (English and Malayalam), type, amount, and the dates for the payment window → tap Save.

---
---

## SECTION 7 — Paying Your Masavari (Members)

1. Tap **Finance** (or tap **My Subscriptions** from the Home page)
2. You will see which months or collections are still outstanding for your family
3. Tap **Pay Now** next to the one you want to pay
4. The church bank account and UPI details are shown — complete your payment through your bank or UPI app
5. Come back to this app and fill in: the amount, the date of payment, and the payment reference number (shown in your bank app as "UTR" or "Transaction ID") — you can also attach a screenshot
6. The status will show **"Submitted — waiting for the Treasurer to check"**
7. Once the Treasurer confirms it, you will get a notification and a receipt number

> If you paid cash at the church office, you do not need to do anything in the app — the Deacon will record it for you.

## Common Questions

**Q: I tapped Save Details but nothing happened.**
Make sure the green ✓ appears on screen. If you see a red message instead, take a screenshot and send it to the church office.

**Q: I cannot see the messages in my group.**
You need to be an approved member of that group. Contact your Group Leader or the church office to be added.

**Q: The app says "Registration Pending" and I cannot get in.**
Contact the church office or the Vicar. Someone from the office needs to approve your account.

**Q: I sent a payment through the app but it still shows as pending.**
The Treasurer will check it within 2 working days. If it has been longer than that, contact the office and give them your bank payment reference number.

**Q: I made a mistake recording a life event. Can I change it?**
No — life events are permanent records. Record a new corrected entry instead. The Vicar can approve corrections through the Approvals page.

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
Yes. The app is built so that each person can only see what they are supposed to see. Your date of birth, email address, and family details are never shown in the public directory.
