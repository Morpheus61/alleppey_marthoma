## 1. First Time Login

> For anyone opening the app for the very first time.

### What you need
- A smartphone or computer with internet access
- Your **Indian mobile number** (the one registered with the church)

### Step-by-step

**Step 1 — Open the App**  
Open the app in your browser or from your home screen (if installed as an app).

**Step 2 — Enter Your Mobile Number**  
Type in your 10-digit Indian mobile number (e.g. `98765 43210`).  
You do **not** need a password. The app uses a one-time SMS code instead.

**Step 3 — Enter the OTP**  
You will receive a **6-digit SMS code** on your phone within a few seconds.  
Type it into the box on screen and tap **Verify**.

**Step 4 — What happens next?**

| Your situation | What happens |
|---|---|
| Your number is already in the church register | Your account is created and waiting for **Admin approval** |
| The Admin pre-loaded your number before launch | You are automatically approved and taken straight to the app |

**Step 5 — Fill in Your Profile** *(if asked)*  
Enter your name, house name, address, and family member details.  
You can also upload a profile photo and a family photo at this point, or later.

**Step 6 — Wait for Approval**  
If your account says **"Pending Approval"**, the Parish Secretary will review and approve it shortly.  
You will be able to use the full app once approved.

> **Tip:** If you already logged in before on this device, the app will take you directly to the home screen — no OTP needed again.

## 2. Parish Members

> For all baptised members and family households.

### What you can do in the app

#### My Profile
- View your membership card with your name, date of birth, address, and family members
- Tap **Edit** to update your details (changes go to the Secretary for review)
- Upload or change your **profile photo** (round photo shown in the directory)
- Upload a **family photo** (shown in the Parish Directory)

#### Parish Directory
- Browse all active church members, grouped by Bhagam (ward)
- Search by name or family name
- Tap a member's name to see their contact details

#### Parish Calendar
- See all upcoming services, prayer meetings, and events
- Get push notification reminders on your phone before events (allow notifications when asked)

#### Groups
- See all parish groups (prayer groups, youth fellowship, committees, etc.)
- Read announcements from your groups in English and Malayalam
- Request to join a group (the group leader will approve)

#### My Subscriptions (Finance)
- See your family's full payment history
- Submit a **UPI or NEFT payment declaration**:
  1. Go to **Finance → Pay Now**
  2. Select the collection (e.g. Masavari for the month)
  3. Scan the church QR code and pay using your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  4. Enter the **UTR / reference number** from your payment
  5. Upload a screenshot of the payment (optional but recommended)
  6. Tap **Submit** — your payment shows as *"Submitted — awaiting verification"*
  7. Once the Treasurer verifies it, you will see the receipt number

> **Note:** Cash payments are recorded by the Deacon — you do not need to do anything for cash.

## 3. Vicar (Super Admin)

> The Vicar has full access to all parts of the app.

### What you can do

#### Approvals Queue
- All changes made by the Secretary, Treasurer, or staff appear here for your review
- You see a clear **before → after** comparison for every change
- Tap **Approve** or **Reject** (with a reason) in 3 taps
- Your own direct changes do not need approval

#### Role Management
- Go to **Admin → Role Management**
- Assign or remove the following roles for any church member:
  - **Secretary (Admin)** — manages members and registry
  - **Treasurer** — handles finance verification and reports
  - **Deacon** — records cash collections
  - **Super Admin** — another Vicar-level account (use with caution)
- Only you can assign or revoke roles
- All role changes are permanently recorded in the audit log

#### Direct Registry Edits
- Edit any household or family member record directly — no change request needed
- Add, correct, or archive life events (baptism, marriage, death, etc.)

#### Funds & Collections Setup
- Create **Funds** (e.g. General Fund, Building Fund)
- Create **Collection Types** (e.g. Masavari monthly subscription, Building Fund appeal)
- Set the amount, collection window (start and end dates), and payment modes

#### Group Leaders
- Only the Vicar can promote a member to Group Leader
- Go to **Admin → Groups**, select the group, and assign a leader

#### Admin Dashboard
- Live overview of total members, pending approvals, active groups, and events this week

## 4. Parish Secretary

> The Secretary manages the membership register, groups, and announcements.

### What you can do

#### Approve New Members
- Go to **Admin → Pending Approvals**
- Review each new registration request
- Tap **Approve** to activate the account, or **Reject** with a reason

#### Add or Import Members

**Single member:**
1. Go to **Admin → Registry → New Household**
2. Fill in the family name (house name), address, Bhagam (ward), and head of family
3. Add each family member: name (English + Malayalam), relation, date of birth, gender
4. Save — the household appears in the registry

**Bulk import (Excel / CSV):**
1. Prepare your spreadsheet (see [Section 7 — Directory Upload Format](#directory-upload-format))
2. Go to **Admin → Members → Import**
3. Upload the file — the app will preview the data before saving
4. Confirm the import

#### Manage Profiles
- Edit any member's name, address, phone, or family details
- Activate, disable, or restore accounts
- Link an app account to a registry family member (so their phone shows with a ✓ tick in the directory)

#### Parish Registry
- View and manage all households grouped by Bhagam
- Add or correct life events for any family member (baptism, confirmation, marriage, death)
- Enrol family members into prayer groups and other groups
- The **Unlinked Profiles** panel shows registered app users not yet linked to a household — link them with one tap

#### Groups & Events
- Create new parish groups and archive inactive ones
- Assign group leaders (only the Vicar can do this — submit a request if needed)
- Create events and post bilingual announcements (English + Malayalam)

#### Parish-Wide Announcements
- Go to **Admin → Posts → New Post**
- Write the message in **English** (required)
- Tap **Draft →** to get a Malayalam version (review and correct it before posting)
- Tick the **"I have reviewed the Malayalam text"** checkbox
- Tap **Post** — it appears in all members' feeds

#### Certificates
- Go to **Admin → Certificates**
- Review certificate requests submitted by members
- Approve or reject each request
- Upload your signature (drawn on screen or image upload) to issue the certificate

> **Note:** Changes to registry records and financial data are sent to the Vicar as a Change Request for approval.

## 5. Parish Treasurer

> The Treasurer manages payment verification and financial reports.

### What you can do

#### Verify UPI / NEFT Payments
1. Go to **Admin → Finance → Verify Payments**
2. Each card shows: family name, collection type, amount, UTR reference number, and screenshot
3. Check that the UTR number matches the payment in your bank / UPI app
4. Tap **Verify ✓** — a receipt number is automatically assigned (e.g. `SGM-D-00001`)
5. Or tap **Reject ✗** — enter a reason (e.g. *"UTR not found"*) — the member is notified

#### Record Cash Payments
1. Go to **Admin → Finance → Cash Entry**
2. Search for the family name
3. Select the collection type and enter the amount
4. Tap **Save** — a receipt number is assigned immediately

#### Finance Dashboard & Reports
- View total collections per fund, per Bhagam, per month
- Compare cash vs UPI/NEFT payments
- Track families with outstanding arrears (unpaid Masavari months)

#### Masavari Arrears (All Bhagams)
- Go to **Admin → Finance → Arrears**
- See **all unpaid months across every Bhagam**, grouped by Bhagam → family
- Each Bhagam shows the total outstanding amount and a breakdown per family
- Export the list to PDF or Excel for deacon collection rounds

> The same full Bhagam-wise arrears view is accessible to the Deacon, Secretary, and Vicar as well.

#### Propose a Reversal (Correction)
- If a payment was recorded incorrectly, go to the entry and tap **Propose Reversal**
- Describe the correction — this goes to the Vicar as a Change Request
- Once the Vicar approves, a correction entry is automatically created (the original is never deleted)

> **Important:** You can also approve Deacon cash entries before they are finalised.

## 6. Deacon / Collection Helper

> The Deacon records cash contributions collected during service or home visits.

### What you can do

#### Record a Cash Collection
1. Go to **Finance → Cash Entry**
2. Search for the family name
3. Select the collection type (e.g. *Masavari — July 2025*)
4. Enter the amount collected
5. Tap **Submit** — the entry is sent to the Treasurer for approval
6. Once the Treasurer approves it, a receipt number is assigned

#### View Masavari Arrears (All Bhagams)
- Go to **Finance → Arrears**
- You can see **all outstanding Masavari dues across every Bhagam** — not just your own ward
- Each Bhagam shows a list of families with unpaid months and the total amount due
- Use this view before a collection round to know exactly which families to visit

> **All management roles** (Deacon, Treasurer, Secretary, Vicar) can view the full parish-wide Masavari arrears list, Bhagam by Bhagam.

#### What you cannot do
- You **cannot** verify or approve payments (Treasurer only)
- You **cannot** edit registry records (Secretary only)
- Your cash entries are **pending** until the Treasurer approves them

### 7.3 Directory Upload Format

To bulk-import existing church members from an Excel or CSV file, prepare the spreadsheet with the following columns.  
**Column names must match exactly** (copy and paste the header row below into your file).

#### Household (Family) Sheet

| Column Name | Required | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| `house_name` | ✅ Yes | Family / house name | Pandampurath |
| `house_name_ml` | Recommended | Malayalam house name | പന്ദംപുരത്ത് |
| `address` | ✅ Yes | Full postal address | Church Road, Alappuzha |
| `bhagam` | ✅ Yes | Ward name (must match Bhagam list) | Thekku Bhagam |
| `phone_landline` | Optional | Landline number with STD code | 0477-2XXXXXX |
| `head_name` | Recommended | Head of family's full name | Motty Philip |

#### Family Members Sheet

| Column Name | Required | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| `house_name` | ✅ Yes | Must match the household `house_name` above | Pandampurath |
| `full_name` | ✅ Yes | Member's full name in English | Motty Philip |
| `full_name_ml` | Recommended | Member's name in Malayalam | മോട്ടി ഫിലിപ്പ് |
| `relation` | ✅ Yes | Relation to head of family | Head / Spouse / Son / Daughter |
| `date_of_birth` | ✅ Yes | Format: **YYYY-MM-DD** | 1975-06-15 |
| `gender` | ✅ Yes | `Male` / `Female` / `Other` | Male |
| `phone` | Recommended | Mobile number — enables auto-approval on first login | 9876543210 |
| `email` | Optional | Email address | example@email.com |

> **Tips:**  
> — Save the file as **CSV (UTF-8)** format to preserve Malayalam characters correctly.  
> — Do not leave the `bhagam` column blank — every family must belong to a ward.  
> — `date_of_birth` must be in `YYYY-MM-DD` format (e.g. `1990-03-21`, not `21/03/1990`).

### 7.6 Funds & Collection Setup

Before collections can be recorded, the Vicar must set up:

1. **Funds** — the ledger buckets money is tracked under (e.g. General Fund, Building Fund)
2. **Masavari Amount** — the fixed monthly subscription amount per family (confirmed by the Vicar)
3. **Collection Windows** — start and end dates for each collection drive

Go to **Admin → Finance → Collections** to create these.
